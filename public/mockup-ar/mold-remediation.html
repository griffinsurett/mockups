Mold Warning Signs

Common Indicators You Should Not Ignore

Persistent musty or earthy odor
Visible spotting on walls, ceilings, or trim
Recurring moisture near plumbing or HVAC zones
Discoloration around windows or behind furniture
Repeated minor leaks in the same areas
Past water damage with incomplete drying
